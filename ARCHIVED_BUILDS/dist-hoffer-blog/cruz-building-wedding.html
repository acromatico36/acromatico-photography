A Wedding in Miami like no other

It’s not every day that you get to run into your past clients, we have to admit, it can be very emotional ::sniff sniff::… In this amazing Cruz Building Wedding, we ran into almost everybody that we knew, such a small world that all our clients were together in one room celebrating Melissa and Alex’s wedding! You might remember these two from their barn engagement session. They finally tied the knot and they really are true sweethearts.  Read more on their story below  👇  👇  👇

Why choose the Cruz Building Wedding as your ultimate venue

When this beautiful Cruz Building Wedding took off, in 3 floors of amazingness, the party was on!  Every single one of the guests gasped as they entered a wedding venue like they’ve never seen before.  We have to say, it was definitely a photographer’s playground, we couldn’t stop taking pictures of every little thing. The place truly speaks for itself! 

This photo was done in the second floor of the Cruz Building Wedding.  So many rich colors and details surround the dining room where the guests entered and were pampered to a fine 5-course meal.

The photo of this cake was taken at the third floor where another amazing space awaits to dance and party like never before.  The colors of the up-lights really light up the ambiance.  You can tell this Cruz Building Wedding couple spared no detail when it came to decorations.

Above is the grand ballroom of this Cruz Building Wedding.  Every single angle provides so much richness to your wedding dress and his charming looks.  Below, we captured the sunset in the balcony on the third floor, right next to the dance floor.

This was their grand entrance, entering from the second floor down to the first, where all the guests were waiting for their first dance.

As a photographer, i always enjoy being able to capture moments like these from the second floor, it just makes everything freeze around while the bride & groom dance.  So romant
