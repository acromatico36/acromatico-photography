Bessy + Justin | Coral Gables Country Club Wedding

January 25, 2013

This bride and groom were one of a kind! Bessy and Justin tied the knot during their beautiful outdoor ceremony at the Coral Gables Country Club. The girls proved they were the ones with the muscles when they carried Justin with their strong, working-out, arms. The white and blue’s in this wedding were amazing, they created a really nice environment. Well, not only did we get to shoot their engagement session in a golf court, but they also decided to add a little bit of golf to their wedding when they did their night shots.
