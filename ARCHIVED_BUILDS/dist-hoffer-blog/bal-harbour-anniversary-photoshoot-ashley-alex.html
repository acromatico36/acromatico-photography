Bal Harbour Anniversary Photoshoot | Ashley + Alex

October 23, 2021

Ashely and Alex decided to celebrate their one year anniversary by capturing their memories at Bal Harbour.

Italo shot their wedding last year during their anniversary (they share the same wedding date!) and we go to capture their love after one year and a pandemic later.

Ashely said she wanted to get some boho vibes and also get in the water. This was a true recipe for BOMB beach photos! We love adventurous couples that are always up for anything. Despite not being a huge fan of the beach, she did this for her hubby and the results were amazing.

It was so nice being able to capture Ashley and Alex in their one year anniversary photoshoot at Bal Harbour beach.

I may or may not have gotten soaked and splashed on but it was totally worth it!
