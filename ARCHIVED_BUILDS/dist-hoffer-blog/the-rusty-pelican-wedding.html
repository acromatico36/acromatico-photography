The Rusty Pelican Wedding

January 22, 2019

You can’t go wrong with The Rusty Pelican as your venue, one of our favorites for sure!  Kelly and Norberto definitely have a very unique taste and everything they envisioned for their wedding day their amazing planner Gabriela Sousa Events made it happen effortlessly, highly recommend her!
