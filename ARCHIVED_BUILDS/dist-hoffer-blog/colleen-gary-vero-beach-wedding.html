Colleen + Gary | Vero Beach Wedding

May 23, 2011

After a 2 hour drive for us and a 2.5 hour flight for them, it was great to finally meet Colleen and Gary! Flying from the beautiful city of New York, they had their destination wedding at the Vero Beach Kimpton Hotel & Spa. It almost seems like in every post we brag about how much fun we had in each wedding, but really, we can’t even imagine complaining about a single wedding! We always seem to leave with a happy face and smiles, and this was certainly a very special occasion. Congratulations to Colleen and Gary, you both very well deserve each other!
