Katia + Matteo | Abruzzo, Italy Wedding

June 21, 2012

When they said “come to Italy for our wedding” we said YES!  We really had been missing Italy for a while, and it’s so good to be back in this beautiful part of the Adriatic Coast. Not only did we enjoy shooting this wedding but we got to pig out. If you didn’t know, Italian weddings last from noon to midnight and you get tons and tons of delicious home-made traditional plates, so it’s hour after hour of eating and partying. This is by far one of our biggest posts! We couldn’t contain our clicks shooting this beautiful couple. You may already know these faces from their engagement session, well, here they are now officially MARRIED! Katia e Matteo: auguri auguri e tanti più auguri ancora! Enjoy our mega post, stay tuned for their after session.
