Viviana + Jahziel | Coral Gables Wedding in Miami

January 21, 2011

Our biggest post yet! Well deserved, as this past weekend we had a spectacular 10-hour coverage at the Women’s Club in Coral Gables. We really enjoyed taking pictures of these two. From the start, Viviana had tons of getting ready details prepared for us, and taking pictures of Jahziel and the guys in Downtown Miami was tons of fun. Most of the guests were in tears at the ceremony, but everyone seemed to really enjoy the night with the non-stop dancing and terrific music the DJ had for them. We hope you enjoy these pictures as much as we did!
