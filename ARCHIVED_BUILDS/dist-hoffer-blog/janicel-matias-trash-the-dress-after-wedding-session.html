Janicel + Matias | Trash-the-dress After Wedding Session

December 16, 2010

Trash the dress…anyone? Yesss!! We finally got someone to do it! And not just anyone, our new sister in law! We had a blast taking pictures of these newlyweds in the beautiful north miami beach area. As most of you know by now, we love to get dirty, on the sand in the mud in the water…and my silly husband, for the SECOND time, got in the water with his brand new iPhone 4. You may recall this same incident only happened 2 months ago with Khadine & Dwayne’s engagement, where he had the perfect excuse to upgrade his dead iPhone 3GS. But gladly, everything turned out to be ok this time and we’re just glad he didn’t drop the camera in the water! Much love and happiness to Janicel & Matias for their new marriage! Congratulations!!!
