Estephania + Lief | Floridian Ballroom Wedding

February 22, 2014

We shoot many weddings, but it’s not every day we get to shoot our dear friend’s wedding. We were thrilled when Estephania and Lief asked us to be a part of their big day! These two have been dating for many loving years and last summer they made it official in New York. They are definitely made for eachother and it really showed in the sweet wedding that they had at the Floridian Ballroom. Lief couldn’t take his eyes of his bride as she sang a lovely Bruno Mars song to her hubby. It was truly touching! Enjoy some of our favorite moments from their beautiful wedding. Congrats again guys, we are so happy for you!
