December 30, 2011

Some friends from Italy came to Florida, so we decided to take them to the Everglades, since we’ve heard so many wonderful things about this place. It was truly amazing! We’ve lived here for so long and never experienced anything like this. We got to see some huge alligators and beautiful wild birds and we even touch a mini alligator! If you ever come to Florida, beside to go to the beautiful beaches this is definitely a MUST see! Enjoy!
