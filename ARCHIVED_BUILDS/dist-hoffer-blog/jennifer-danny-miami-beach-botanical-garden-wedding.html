Jennifer + Danny | Miami Beach Botanical Garden Wedding

April 2, 2012

We couldn’t meet Jennifer and Danny before their wedding day, but they sounded so cool and fun over the phone and they had so many wonderful ideas for the day that we had no doubt it was going to be an awesome wedding full of awesome people. We weren’t wrong! The wedding was a total success. We stared the day at the beautiful Viceroy hotel in Brickell where they were getting ready, and ended at the Miami Beach Botanical Garden where they did their ceremony and celebrated with a beautiful reception.  It truly was a beautiful day! We wish you guys the best of the best on your marriage! Enjoy a few of our favorites shots of the day.
