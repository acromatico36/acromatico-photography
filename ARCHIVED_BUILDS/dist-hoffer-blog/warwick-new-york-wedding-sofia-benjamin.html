Warwick New York Wedding | Sofia + Benjamin

March 30, 2020

My little brother got married! YAY! Meet Benjamin and his gorgeous wife Sofia.. They decided to have a destination wedding in Warwick, New York where Benja has spent the last few years. We honestly thought they where crazy by choosing a day in February (29th to be specific) and having all their guest fly from our beautiful tropical weather in Florida to freeze in New York, but we were far from wrong! This was the most EPIC wedding and everything came out just perfect! even the timing was perfect as this was the last wedding the venue had before the coronavirus outbreak. From the days prior to the wedding to the day of everything was INCREDIBLE! They took us to this gorgeous castle to take some pictures and let me tell you I have never seen a bride as brave as Sofia.. I mean it was 20 degrees and she was slaying her sleeveless dress like a queen.. they were both troopers! Thank you guys for thinking even of the smallest details and love you put into your wedding, you made us all feel so special.
