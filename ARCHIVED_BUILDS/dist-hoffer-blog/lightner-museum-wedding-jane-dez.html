Lightner Museum Wedding | Jane + Dez

April 5, 2022

After 20 years of creating moments and memories for beautiful people, I don’t think that we’ve ever taken so many photos in any wedding, ever!

I still can’t believe that it’s only been a year since Jane + Dez got engaged at the Secret Gardens in Miami and now we are here, witnessing one of the most important days of their lives at the Lightner Museum in St. Augustine.

As a destination wedding, the day started at the Embassy Suites by Hilton St Augustine Beach Oceanfront Resort which we got to enjoy so much, it was so nice and chill with that beautiful beach view and resort-style pool.

This wedding was also very special for us, and each family member, especially the parents and siblings made us feel as part of their family. This is why we love working with clients that truly appreciate photography. It inspires us to connect deeply with their memories, and create those moments that last a lifetime.

Jane is one of the most stunning brides that we’ve ever photographed. The team that helped her get glammed is truly amazing and we highly recommend them for any bride wanting that flawless look.

And what can we say about him? Denzel is one of the sweetest guys that we’ve ever met! Completely in love with Jane, supporting her throughout the entire process, a true gentleman, and always attentive to everyone around him (Jane, you really got a good one here!)

We are honored and excited to share this preview of the many photos that we took during your special day. Stay tuned on Acromatico’s Instagram page for behind the scenes!
