New York Indian Wedding | Geeta + Vir

August 4, 2020

I don’t even know where to start with this amazing Indian Wedding we got to shoot in Glen Cove, Long Island. Everyone was just full of life, celebration and color explosions (literally!)

This wedding was so unique on its own and it’s definitely one I will never forget. Right in the middle of the pandemic, Geeta and Vir had their very close family members join them in their forever union as husband and wife. This Sikh-Punjab wedding was very memorable as Geeta was breaking traditions and boundaries. She rode her own horse during her very own Baraat. Needless to say, it was extremely epic. Smoke bombs going everywhere and this rad bride just rode her horse in style: as epic as can be!

One thing I’ve learned about Geeta is she’s not afraid to be who she is, she’s very true to herself and fearless. Combined with Vir, there won’t ever be any limits for these two.

It was such a pleasure shooting this amazing wedding with Heather. After being covered up, masked up, tripping up the stairs (yes, my camera survived) this for sure will be a wedding I won’t forget.

I also can’t forget to give some shot outs to some of the most amazing poeple we got to work with for this wedding:

  • Amee and her team at Karma Events (best wedding planner EVER!)
  • Williamson and his awesome team for killing the cinematography with Park Street Weddings
  • Hair and makeup for thee most beautiful bride: Khadija
  • The amazing caterers at Moghul Express
