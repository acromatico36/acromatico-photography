Liam | Newborn Portrait Session

February 14, 2013

Today, we have someone special: he decided to be a model at only 5 weeks old! Liam is not only our cutest, youngest, little client ever but he’s also one of our great friend’s/past client’s newborn baby. When Susie and Alex called us a week ago to shoot their baby, we couldn’t say no! After we met him, we couldn’t take our lenses off of him (literally)! We fell in love with his blueish-gray eyes, he’s such a handsome little man. Even though took a little while to get comfy with the camera, after a few cries, bottles and screams, the camera LOVED him (and he loved the camera).
