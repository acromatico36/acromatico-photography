Daniela + Arnaud | The Addison Boca Raton Wedding

December 17, 2016

What do Switzerland and Venezuela have in common? Germany! That is where this gorgeous couple met and started the beginning of their beautiful international love story. Daniela and Arnaud were trying to learn the German language when they met at school. This amazing love story flourished and made Arnaud follow her back to the states where they later tied the knot. A day full of emotions, happiness and joy. Their wedding took place at The Addison in Boca Raton. This amazing destination wedding with people from all over the world is living proof of all the family and friends that love this couple so much to make it all the way here for their special day.
