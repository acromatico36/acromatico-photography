Michelle + Oscar | The Biltmore - Renaissance Wedding

September 3, 2011

Hands down, this is one of our most awesome wedding of the year. We were particularly very excited to take pictures of Michelle and Chacho’s (as his friends call him) wedding looking back at their vintage engagement session. We started their getting ready pictures at the Biltmore Hotel in Coral Gables, following their ceremony and then reception at the Renaissance hotel in Miami. She had an amazing dress and stunning looks, without leaving Oscar behind with his charming ways and love they have for each other. To top it off, we got to see ten of our past clients, which made us feel so excited. Time flies by so fast, as it feels it was only yesterday they got married. Congratulations to Michelle & Oscar for tying the knot, and welcome to the best stage of your new lives together!
