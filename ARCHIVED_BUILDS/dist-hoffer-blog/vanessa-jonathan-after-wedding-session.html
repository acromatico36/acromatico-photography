Vanessa + Jonathan | After Wedding Session

May 19, 2010

We had the pleasure of shooting Vanessa and Jonathan’s after wedding session in downtown Miami. We hit Bayside afterwards, which is Miami’s most turistic shopping plaza with live music and great food. Thanks for such a wonderful time!
