Hugh Taylor Birch Park Family Session | Visbal Family

November 28, 2021

For our yearly family photo session with the Visbal Family, we made sure to get everything booked and set for them way ahead of time. Nothing was going to get in the way of their 2021 session…except when it comes to rain. It ALWAYS gets in the way.

Despite having to reschedule their Hugh Taylor Birch Park Family Session twice…it’s true what they say about the third time…except, it wasn’t a charm! Our actual rescheduled, photoshoot raincheck date, ended up raining as well! Thanks to these troopers, we decided to chance it, and we’re SO glad we did!

When we arrived to the Hugh Taylor Birch Park for their family session, clouds were nice and clear and we were able to get their memories captured. What’s even more special about this place is the last time I was here, little Olivia was still in the oven! It’s crazy how time flies and it’s so beautiful to get to see our clients on a year to year basis and seeing their little families grow.
