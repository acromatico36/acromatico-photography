This bride wasn’t afraid to get rained on and her and her groom had a gorgeous indoor wedding ceremony at the Benvenuto Restaurant in Boynton Beach. Everything turned out beautifully! Later, Chelsea and Jim partied like rockstars! The food was delicious, the party was pump, everything was amazing!
