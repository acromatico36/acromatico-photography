Angela + John | Vizcaya Engagement Session

June 19, 2012

This E session was almost a live proposal! Let us tell you the story: John and Angela flew all the way from New York to celebrate their engagement. He was planning on proposing during the trip! Too bad Angela figured something was up and John had to jump to plan B, proposing back home. The good thing is she said yes! They are such a wonderful, dynamic and fun couple to work with! The day was gorgeous, but Florida doesn’t give out sunny days for free. John had to suffer in his long sleeve shirt and tight jeans, but he didn’t mind. Angela, on the other hand, had it easy: she rocked her summer dress and awesome shoes like a true fashionista! It was literally a walk in the park for her.
