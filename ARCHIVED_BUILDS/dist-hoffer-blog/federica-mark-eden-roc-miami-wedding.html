federica + mark | eden roc miami wedding

October 14, 2011

What an amazing weekend we had. You might remember Federica and Mark from their engagement session in Lincoln road. They had friends and family coming from Italy and Canada to witness their beautiful wedding at the Eden Roc Renaissance in Miami Beach. We were afraid that it was going to continue raining the whole day, but fortunately the skies cleared up allowing us to get some nice outside pictures, thank you Florida!

If you’ve ever been to an Italian wedding expect nothing less than great food, lots of dancing and tons of fun! Enjoy the post!
