Bessy + Justin | Cauley Square Maternity Session

July 22, 2014

We started a journey with Bessy and Justin when they were only engaged. Got to experience shooting their wedding, and now, the arrival of their baby in their maternity session. What a privilege to have been able to shoot these guys 3 times in a row! They chose to do a little beachy theme in their very own backyard. Later on , we went to the Historic Village of Cauley Square in Miami to finish off. We can’t get enough of these pictures and Bessy was truly a great mommy-to-be-model in her 7 months out, pregnancy. After almost 2 years of marriage, these two look amazing! Congrats again guys on your baby, we wish you the very best on your growing family. Enjoy some of our faves!
