Family Photo Shoot at Villa del Balbianello | Lake Como Italy

October 31, 2023

Have you ever dreamed of visiting a fairy-tale villa on the shores of a beautiful lake? That’s exactly what we did when we went to Villa del Balbianello, one of the most marvelous places on Lake Como. We were fortunate enough to have our dear friends and gorgeous Artiles family join us for this unforgettable experience. And of course, we couldn’t miss the opportunity to capture some stunning photos of our happy moments together.

Villa del Balbianello is not just a regular villa. It is a movie star. Literally. This magnificent mansion has been featured in some of the most iconic films of all time, such as Star Wars: Episode II – Attack of the Clones and Casino Royale. You might remember the scenes where Anakin and Padme got married, or where James Bond recovered from his injuries. Well, those scenes were filmed right here, at this very spot.

As soon as we arrived at the villa, we felt like we were transported to another world. A world of fantasy, romance, and adventure. A world where anything could happen. We couldn’t believe our eyes when we saw the breathtaking views of the lake and the mountains, the elegant architecture of the villa, and the lush garden that surrounded it. We felt like we were part of a movie ourselves.

And that’s exactly how we wanted to capture our photos. We wanted to make them look like movie posters, with dramatic angles, vibrant colors, and expressive poses. We wanted to show their personality and emotions, as well as our admiration for this place. We wanted to tell a story with our images, a story of friendship, fun, and love.

We had so much fun exploring the villa and finding the best spots for our photos. We took photos from the lake, from the garden, from the loggia, and from inside the villa. We tried to recreate some of the scenes from the movies, as well as invent some of our own. We made memories that will last forever.

We hope you enjoyed reading about the Artiles family photo shoot at Villa del Balbianello. We had an amazing time there, and we are so thankful for our friends who shared this experience with us. If you ever have a chance to visit Lake Como, don’t miss this incredible place. You will feel like a movie star yourself.

If you are planning to do a photo shoot at Villa del Balbianello, here are some tips that might help you:

    • Book your visit in advance: The villa is open to the public from March to November, but it can get very busy during peak season. You need to book your tickets online or by phone at least a day before your visit. You can also book a guided tour if you want to learn more about the history and the art of the villa.
    • Choose the best time: The best time to take photos at the villa is either early in the morning or late in the afternoon, when the light is softer and more flattering. Avoid midday, when the sun is too bright and harsh. Also, try to avoid weekends and holidays, when the villa is more crowded and noisy.
    • Take a boat: The villa is accessible by land or by water, but we recommend taking a boat if you want to enjoy the full beauty of the lake and the villa. You can rent a boat from one of the nearby towns, such as Lenno or Tremezzo, or hire a private driver who can take you there and wait for you while you visit. The boat ride will also give you some amazing photo opportunities along the way.
    • Explore different angles: The villa offers many different perspectives and backgrounds for your photos. You can take photos from the lake, from the garden, from the loggia, or from inside the villa. Try to capture both wide shots and close-ups, both portraits and landscapes, both candid and posed moments. Experiment with different angles and compositions, and have fun with your creativity.
    • Enjoy yourself: The most important tip for taking great photos at Villa del Balbianello is to enjoy yourself and have fun with your family and friends. Don’t worry too much about getting the perfect shot or following all the rules. Just relax and let your emotions and expressions shine through. The best photos are those that reflect your personality and your happiness.

We hope you enjoyed reading about our family photo shoot at Villa del Balbianello. We had a wonderful time there, and we are so grateful for our friends who shared this adventure with us. If you ever have a chance to visit Lake Como, don’t miss this magical place. You will fall in love with it, just like we did.
