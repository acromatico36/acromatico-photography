Aviva + Patrick | The Rusty Pelican Wedding

July 18, 2012

Rain rain go away…This should be the title for this last Sunday’s wedding. Despite the rainstorm that was coming our way at the beautiful Rusty Pelican in Key Biscayne, Aviva and Patrick still got married in the beautiful terrace with the amazing Miami skyline views. We have to say she’s a champ for taking a few drops in her wedding gown and proceeding with the ceremony. Despite the crazy weather, she got what she dreamed of on her special day! This wedding was a particular one since we got married in the same exact place! We got to relive our memories from almost 3 years ago.
