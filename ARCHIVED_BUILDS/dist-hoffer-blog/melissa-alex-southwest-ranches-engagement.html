Melissa + Alex | Southwest Ranches Engagement

February 5, 2013

If you love horses like we do, you’re definitely going to love this session! Melissa and Alex chose the footloose, country-cowboy theme for their engagement session. We went to a beautiful barn: World Class Equestrian Center, located in Southwest Ranches. It was surrounded with gorgeous horses…we even got to play with them! It was so much fun shooting these two. They are very fun and outgoing, and yes, they both have the good looks! We couldn’t wait any longer to show you a sneak peek of their session. Don’t they look hot? Well, please be the judge!
