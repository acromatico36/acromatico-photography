Boho Senior Portrait, Bal Harbour | Jocelyn

December 7, 2021

Senior sessions don’t come too too often for us, but when they do, we LOVE them.

We got to shoot Jocelyn’s boho senior portrait sesh at Bal Harbour Beach, one of our favorite beach locations to shoot at.

Working with Jocelyn was so refreshing and inspiring. Despite never having met her before, we felt an instant connection and my camera sure felt it! I asked her to give me a little vision of what she wanted and the rest was so easy. Capturing her contagious, energetic, free-spirited personality was a breeze. People like her make us love what we do even more!

After many laughs, saving turtles (we didn’t really save them we just ran after a plastic bag that would totally end up in one of their noses, so we technically did #savetheturtles), getting soaked and almost caught a cold later, here you have it, Jocelyn’s boho senior portrait.

It was such a pleasure working with you, this will be for sure one of my most memorable photoshoots. Thanks for the inspiration and for trusting me with your vision!

*Disclaimer*

We were NOT supposed to get her fully wet. After all, it is technically “winter” in Florida so the water at 5pm is totally not warm anymore. Told her to just lay down (by the water) and tilt her head back…when a whole wave snatched her in. Despite getting SOAKED, she went with it and turned this session into this.
