Weston Florida Wedding | Keila + Jonny

May 7, 2019

Can’t believe how quickly time flies.  Literally, it feels like yesterday since we met our childhood friend Jonny.  To watch him grow up and meet his beautiful bride and wife-to-be, it’s been a special feeling.

Keila and Jonny started their Weston Country Club wedding in the outside gardens facing the endless green views of the golf course.  For us, it was nothing less than a blast to follow them in a golf cart racing at what felt like 20 miles an hour (maybe it was those downhill slopes or you could feel more speed while standing behind the cart while shooting in movement!).  Her gorgeous hair design was created by super talented Jany Millo Carrasco from Miami Bridal Hair.  Their venue was carefully decorated with light accents that lit up the place.  And our friend Veejay the Deejay kept the Weston florida wedding going with his contagious music.  We wish you guys a happy life, marriage is awesome!
