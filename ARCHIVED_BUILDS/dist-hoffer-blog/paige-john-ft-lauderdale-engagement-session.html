Paige + John | Ft. Lauderdale Engagement Session

March 9, 2013

It’s not every day you get to shoot a couple from Pennsylvania. Paige and John recently flew all the way down to Florida to catch some rays and run away from the cold. These lovebirds met back home in college. While John was a football player, Paige didn’t seem to care a big about his impressive athlete skills. After playing hard to get for so long, John finally won her heart. These two are tying the knot this September back home in Pennsylvania in the same barn that they reconstructed and turned into a Reception Hall. Very impressive huh?
