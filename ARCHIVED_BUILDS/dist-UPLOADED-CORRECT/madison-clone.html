Madison & Jordan | Cork Factory Hotel Wedding

Madison and Jordan's Cork Factory Hotel wedding was nothing short of magical. From the romantic first look to the emotional ceremony, every moment was filled with love and joy. The Cork Factory Hotel provided the perfect industrial-chic backdrop for their celebration, with its exposed brick walls and stunning natural light.

Wedding Portraits at Cork Factory

The couple took advantage of the beautiful architecture and natural light throughout the Cork Factory Hotel for their portraits. Every corner of this venue offers unique photo opportunities, from the intimate indoor spaces to the charming outdoor areas.

The Reception

As the evening progressed, the celebration moved into full swing with heartfelt toasts, joyful dancing, and unforgettable moments shared with family and friends. The Cork Factory Hotel's elegant reception space was transformed into a romantic setting that perfectly captured Madison and Jordan's vision for their special day.
