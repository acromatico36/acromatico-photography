Surprise Proposal Sarasota | A Romantic Moment to Remember

When Isander reached out to me about planning a surprise proposal at the beautiful Selby Gardens in Sarasota, I knew we were in for something special. The gardens provide the perfect backdrop for romantic moments, with lush tropical plants, serene water features, and hidden corners perfect for an intimate proposal.

Isander wanted to create a moment that Natalie would never forget. We carefully planned every detail, from the timing to ensure perfect lighting, to the exact spot where he would get down on one knee. The anticipation was building as we waited for the perfect moment.

The Perfect Moment

As they walked through the gardens, I positioned myself discreetly to capture every emotion. When Isander got down on one knee, Natalie's face lit up with pure joy and surprise. The golden hour light filtering through the palm trees created a magical atmosphere that you can see in these photos.

After the proposal, we spent some time capturing their happiness and excitement. The gardens offered countless beautiful spots for portraits, and the newly engaged couple was glowing with love and joy.

Planning Your Own Surprise Proposal

If you're planning a surprise proposal in Sarasota, Selby Gardens is an incredible location. The variety of settings within the gardens means you'll have plenty of options for both the proposal moment and follow-up photos. The best times to visit are early morning or late afternoon for the most beautiful light.

Whether you're planning a proposal, engagement session, or wedding photography in the Sarasota area, I'd love to help you capture these precious moments. Feel free to reach out to discuss your vision!
