Rustic Barn Wedding at Rolling Meadow Farm

Sade & Luke | October 15, 2024

Sade and Luke's rustic barn wedding at Rolling Meadow Farm was everything a fall celebration should be - warm, intimate, and absolutely stunning. The golden autumn light filtered through the barn's wooden beams, creating the perfect atmosphere for their special day.

The Venue

Rolling Meadow Farm offers the perfect blend of rustic charm and modern elegance. The restored barn features soaring ceilings with exposed wooden beams, creating an intimate yet spacious feel for the celebration.
