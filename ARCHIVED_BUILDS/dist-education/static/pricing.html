❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What age is this curriculum designed for?

The Acromatico Creator Academy is designed for kids ages 7-14. The curriculum progressively builds skills from basic visual concepts to advanced portfolio creation, making it perfect for homeschool families.

Do students need any equipment to start?

Students can start with just a smartphone camera! As they progress, we recommend a basic digital camera or DSLR, but it's not required to begin the journey.

Can my child learn at their own pace?

Absolutely! While the curriculum is structured month-by-month, students have lifetime access and can move faster or slower based on their interest and schedule. There's no pressure!

Is there a refund policy?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the curriculum within the first 30 days, we'll refund your payment in full, no questions asked.

Can I enroll multiple children?

Yes! One subscription covers your entire family. Create separate student profiles for each child from your parent dashboard, and track their individual progress.
