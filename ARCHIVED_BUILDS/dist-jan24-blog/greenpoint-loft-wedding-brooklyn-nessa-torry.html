Greenpoint Loft Wedding – Brooklyn | Nessa + Torry

April 19, 2022 • New York, Brooklyn

Just a few months ago, we were together with Nessa and Torry for their engagement session. By the time the session ended, we nearly froze to death. Clearly, the cold loves us. Despite spring finally springing, it was a nice and cool day on this Brooklyn wedding day.

Before heading to The Williamsburg Hotel we did a pit stop at Upstate Stock to get fueled up for the day. It’s our fave! Nessa awaited us at her gorgeous suite with the best city views and a beautiful smile. She was as zen as she could be!

If you’re getting married or are undecided about doing a first look, keep scrolling, you might change your mind! We are all in when it comes to first looks. You get to have an intimate moment, get a really awesome groom reaction AND you get bonus time added to your wedding day timeline! How? Once the first look is in, we get to squeeze in all the bridal and couple photos that usually we tend to rush for after the ceremony. What’s the big deal anyway? – you might ask – Well, you get to have MORE couple photos and you don’t risk the chance of loosing the beautiful daylight if things run behind.

Nessa and Torry did just that! We went to The Boathouse at Prospect Park for their first look. This bride left her groom speechless, even shedding a few tears! We’re not crying, you’re crying!

After this, we headed to the Greenpoint Loft where they had their rustic, boho, eclectic ceremony and reception. I do have to say, if you’re looking for amazing New York skyline views, this is the place! Keep scrolling to get a little taste of Nessa and Torry’s wedding. There were even Nigerian traditional outfits for this one!

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?
    We recommend booking 12-18 months in advance for peak season dates (May-October). Popular weekends book up quickly, especially for destination locations. Off-season dates (November-April) may have more flexibility, but it's best to secure your photographer as soon as your venue is booked.
    What should we look for when choosing a wedding photographer?
    Look for a style that resonates with you, experience shooting at your venue type, a strong portfolio showing consistent quality, clear communication, backup equipment, and backup plans. Review full galleries, not just highlight reels, to see the complete story they tell.
    How long does it take to receive our wedding photos?
    Full edited galleries are typically delivered within 6-8 weeks of your wedding date. We provide sneak peek images within 48-72 hours so you can share the excitement with family and friends right away. Rush delivery may be available for an additional fee.
    Do you shoot destination weddings?
    Yes! We love traveling for weddings and have experience shooting in destinations worldwide. Travel fees vary by location, but we're always excited to explore new venues and capture your story wherever it takes us.
    What happens if you're sick on our wedding day?
    We maintain a network of professional photographers we trust. If an emergency prevents us from shooting your wedding, we'll immediately arrange a qualified replacement at no additional cost to you. This has never happened in our years of shooting, but we have backup plans in place.
