Frequently Asked Questions

How long is an engagement photo session?

Our engagement sessions typically last 1-2 hours, which gives us plenty of time to capture a variety of poses and locations without feeling rushed. This timeframe allows you to relax, be yourselves, and create authentic images you'll cherish forever.

What should we wear for engagement photos in Gold Coast Railroad Engagement Session?

We recommend coordinating outfits that reflect your personal style while complementing Gold Coast Railroad Engagement Session's atmosphere. Solid colors or subtle patterns photograph beautifully. We'll send you a detailed style guide with outfit suggestions once your session is booked.

Can we bring props to our engagement session?

Absolutely! Props that reflect your relationship - like your favorite books, coffee cups, or a vintage car - can add personality to your photos. We love when couples incorporate meaningful items that tell their unique story.
