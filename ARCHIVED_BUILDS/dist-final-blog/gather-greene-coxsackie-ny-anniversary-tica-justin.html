Gather Greene – Coxsackie, NY, Anniversary | Tica + Justin

October 18, 2020

What do you do when you’re in the middle of a pandemic and need to forget that you’re still in 2020 and it happens to be your 7th anniversary?

Easy. You go to Gather Greene.

*** BRIDES, SURE YOU READ TIL THE END ***

These lux, middle of the woods, tiny, woodsy, scandinavian, artsy, boho, cabins are all you need. AND they’re pet friendly. What else can you possibly want?

Oh yeah, peace, quiet and someone to spit stars up on the sky. Yep. It’s got that too! Gather Greene is magic. Highly recommend this for any occasion. Get away, reconnect and live again.

Booking this place was very easy and safe. They take amazing precautions to ensure everyone’s safety. They’re only booking a few cabins at a time and everything gets highly, medically, disinfected as well as aerated. The check in process is 100% contactless. No overcrowding and zero overlapping with others.

The design of this place is phenomenal, my favorite part is the pavilion. You can catch the sunset and forget everything. There’s even little deer that get so SO close. That’s how untouched Gather Greene is. Oh, you might even hear a little wolf pack out in the woods. No biggie..lol!

We had thee most phenomenal anniversary where we were able to forget the world, disconnect from our phones, social media, zoom, facetime and all the digital madness of 2020. It was truly a freeing unforgettable experience. These tiny, glamping, cabins are for sure a great way to be able to be out in the sticks, unplug, while still being able to connect with the earth. We even snuck in a little photo shoot with our furry baby, Valentino. I mean, how could we not? Shot out to our tripod for hooking us up.

OK BRIDES. If you’re still reading. GET THIS:

**GATHER GREENE IS ALSO A WEDDING VENUE**

You can book the pavilion, book the cabins and have a cozy, intimate, wedding in this magical, whimsical, boho, spot. Your guests can lodge here (or not) and the best part it’s only minutes from Athens, Catskill and Hudson. There’s plenty of little spots to go to while you’re away from your routine. It’s close enough to be far enough and the best part is it’s right in our backyards. If you need any sort of information or guidance make sure to contact Jackie, she’s my new go-to, extremely helpful and awesome!

Thank you Gather Greene for an amazing time and for the BOMB pour over! Oh, Valentino says: woof woof woof…(10 out of 10)

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?
    We recommend booking 12-18 months in advance for peak season dates (May-October). Popular weekends book up quickly, especially for destination locations. Off-season dates (November-April) may have more flexibility, but it's best to secure your photographer as soon as your venue is booked.
    What should we look for when choosing a wedding photographer?
    Look for a style that resonates with you, experience shooting at your venue type, a strong portfolio showing consistent quality, clear communication, backup equipment, and backup plans. Review full galleries, not just highlight reels, to see the complete story they tell.
    How long does it take to receive our wedding photos?
    Full edited galleries are typically delivered within 6-8 weeks of your wedding date. We provide sneak peek images within 48-72 hours so you can share the excitement with family and friends right away. Rush delivery may be available for an additional fee.
    Do you shoot destination weddings?
    Yes! We love traveling for weddings and have experience shooting in destinations worldwide. Travel fees vary by location, but we're always excited to explore new venues and capture your story wherever it takes us.
    What happens if you're sick on our wedding day?
    We maintain a network of professional photographers we trust. If an emergency prevents us from shooting your wedding, we'll immediately arrange a qualified replacement at no additional cost to you. This has never happened in our years of shooting, but we have backup plans in place.
