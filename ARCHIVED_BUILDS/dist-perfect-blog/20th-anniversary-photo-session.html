20th Anniversary Photo Session | Mares Family

May 13, 2023 • Miami

It’s not every day that we get to celebrate 20 years of love.  We had the honor of capturing some beautiful moments for our dear friends, the Mares family, who were celebrating their 20th anniversary. 

We chose Matheson Hammock Park in Miami as the location for their photo session, and it was a perfect choice. The park offers a variety of scenic backgrounds, from lush greenery to serene water views. The golden hour light was magical, and the breeze was refreshing.  Claire took this special occasion to use her wedding dress, and she looked truly stunning!  We hope that you enjoy this beautiful 20th anniversary photo session as much as we did.  

And P.S. If you’re planning on taking photos at the Matheson Hammock Park, we’ve left some tips for you at the footer of this blog.

Here are some tips for taking photos at Matheson Hammock Park:

  • Plan ahead and check the weather forecast. The park can get crowded on weekends and holidays, so try to avoid peak hours. Also, be prepared for mosquitoes and bring some repellent.
  • Explore different spots and angles. The park has many hidden gems, such as a coral rock gazebo, a historic coral stone building, and a winding trail. Don’t be afraid to get creative and try different perspectives.
  • If you’re a photographer, use natural light and reflectors. The park has plenty of natural light, especially during sunrise and sunset. You can use reflectors to bounce the light and fill in the shadows. Avoid using flash, as it can create harsh shadows and ruin the mood.
  • Have fun and be yourself. The most important thing is to enjoy the moment and let your personality shine. Don’t worry too much about posing or smiling. Just relax and have fun with your loved ones.

I hope you enjoyed this blog post and got some inspiration for your next anniversary photo session or special occasion. If you are looking for a professional photographer in Miami, feel free to contact us. We would love to work with you and create some amazing memories.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?
We recommend booking 12-18 months in advance for peak season dates (May-October). Popular weekends book up quickly, especially for destination locations. Off-season dates (November-April) may have more flexibility, but it's best to secure your photographer as soon as your venue is booked.
What should we look for when choosing a wedding photographer?
Look for a style that resonates with you, experience shooting at your venue type, a strong portfolio showing consistent quality, clear communication, backup equipment, and backup plans. Review full galleries, not just highlight reels, to see the complete story they tell.
How long does it take to receive our wedding photos?
Full edited galleries are typically delivered within 6-8 weeks of your wedding date. We provide sneak peek images within 48-72 hours so you can share the excitement with family and friends right away. Rush delivery may be available for an additional fee.
Do you shoot destination weddings?
Yes! We love traveling for weddings and have experience shooting in destinations worldwide. Travel fees vary by location, but we're always excited to explore new venues and capture your story wherever it takes us.
What happens if you're sick on our wedding day?
We maintain a network of professional photographers we trust. If an emergency prevents us from shooting your wedding, we'll immediately arrange a qualified replacement at no additional cost to you. This has never happened in our years of shooting, but we have backup plans in place.
