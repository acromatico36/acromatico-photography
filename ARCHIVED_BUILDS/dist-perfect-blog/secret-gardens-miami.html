Secret Gardens Miami: A Guide for your photography portraits

This hidden gem is a photographer’s paradise. It is a great place to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and enjoy some peace and quiet.

In this blog post, we will take you on a tour of the Secret Gardens in Miami. We will show you a gorgeous proposal that we got to witness, and give you some tips on how to take great photos while you are there.

What makes the Secret Gardens in Miami so special?

The 6-acre property, with its lush vegetation, six crystalline waterfalls, and koi-filled ponds that house three breathtaking swans, is a once-in-a-lifetime backdrop.

Throughout the beautiful gardens you can also find various sets, stone arches, and bridges that you can use to enhance your unique shots.

Each turn you make on the property provides a different view, allowing for a vast variety of scenery, making the Secret Gardens Miami one of the top photography location in South Florida.

The best time to take your photos

The best time to take your photos at the Secret Gardens Miami is early in the morning or late in the afternoon. This is when the light is soft and the gardens are less crowded.

We recommend planning to take photos during the week, to avoid less crowds and have more ample space for yourself. And by all means, we highly recommend renting the canoe or any of the props that they offer. It will definitely make your portrait photo session very unique!

Photography tips for shooting at the Secret Gardens in Miami

When taking photos in the Secret Gardens in Miami, be sure to take advantage of the lush vegetation. Use plants and flowers as foreground elements in your photos to add depth and interest.

And don’t forget to explore the hidden nooks and crannies of the garden. This is where you will find the most interesting subjects for your photos.

How much is the Secret Gardens Miami rental for photo shoots?

Their photography permit rental rates are $255 and include:

Three hours of access to the entire Secret Gardens Miami property, including the Villa Laguna. You will have a private, air-conditioned dressing room as well as sitting areas for guests.

Additionally, sets are available to use at no additional cost and private bathrooms are also included. You’re welcome bring the photographer of your choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time of day for engagement photos?
Golden hour - the hour before sunset - provides the most flattering, soft natural light. We can also shoot during "blue hour" just after sunset for a different mood. Avoid midday harsh sunlight which creates unflattering shadows.
What should we wear for our engagement session?
Choose outfits that make you feel confident and reflect your style. Coordinate colors without exact matching. Solid colors photograph better than busy patterns. Bring layers or a second outfit for variety. Most importantly, wear something comfortable that you can move in naturally.
Where should we take our engagement photos?
The best locations are meaningful to your relationship - where you met, got engaged, or love to spend time together. We can also suggest scenic spots based on your style preferences: urban, natural, beachfront, etc. Location scouting is included in our process.
