Arlington, MA – Micro Wedding | Elle + Hector

November 27, 2020 • New York

A New York Wedding. That’s what they initially had planned.

Many of our dear brides have had to really change their wedding plans, pushing dates, having smaller weddings, sacrificing wedding parties, honeymoon and dear ones not being there.

In the case of Elle and Hector, they had to do all that while having to change all their prepaid wedding plans and move their wedding all the way to Massachusetts. When we found out their wedding turned into a destination, micro wedding, we couldn’t be even more excited to be a part of their big day.

They got ready at the Archer Hotel in Burlington, MA. The industrial vibes and the amazing decor was truly a photography playground. To make the most of daylight, I recommended doing a first look. It was sooooo worth it (we’re not crying, you’re crying and so were they!)

This really helped take advantage of some natural light (while we still had it). Sunset at 4:20pm is not fair but we made the best of it and the best use of our light while we could!

Despite this being a micro wedding, over 500 of their family and friends attended live via Zoom. It all turned out to be one big virtual party with a virtual DJ and all. Nothing was going to stop Elle and Hector from partying on their big day.

Also, let’s take a moment to focus on the flowers arrangements. I mean, they were a complete show stopper. Once again, @juniperglenn_ killed it with the flower decor. Pro tip: flowers really make a difference. Don’t you ever skip them! They bring life and color to the room and truly make a huge difference in the whole ambiance. They also brightened up this whole blog post.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?
    We recommend booking 12-18 months in advance for peak season dates (May-October). Popular weekends book up quickly, especially for destination locations. Off-season dates (November-April) may have more flexibility, but it's best to secure your photographer as soon as your venue is booked.
    What should we look for when choosing a wedding photographer?
    Look for a style that resonates with you, experience shooting at your venue type, a strong portfolio showing consistent quality, clear communication, backup equipment, and backup plans. Review full galleries, not just highlight reels, to see the complete story they tell.
    How long does it take to receive our wedding photos?
    Full edited galleries are typically delivered within 6-8 weeks of your wedding date. We provide sneak peek images within 48-72 hours so you can share the excitement with family and friends right away. Rush delivery may be available for an additional fee.
    Do you shoot destination weddings?
    Yes! We love traveling for weddings and have experience shooting in destinations worldwide. Travel fees vary by location, but we're always excited to explore new venues and capture your story wherever it takes us.
    What happens if you're sick on our wedding day?
    We maintain a network of professional photographers we trust. If an emergency prevents us from shooting your wedding, we'll immediately arrange a qualified replacement at no additional cost to you. This has never happened in our years of shooting, but we have backup plans in place.
