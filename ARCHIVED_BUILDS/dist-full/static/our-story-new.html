Meet Ale & Italo

We're Ale & Italo, the award-winning photography and filmmaking duo behind Acromatico. Our journey started in the hills of Abruzzo, Italy, but our work has taken us around the world—from the streets of New York to the beaches of South Florida, and everywhere in between.

We believe in capturing real moments, real energy, and real emotion. No forced poses, no cookie-cutter shots. Just you, living your story, while we document it with an artistic eye and an unfiltered approach.

Our work has been featured by Apple, Martha Stewart, The Knot, WeddingWire, Style Me Pretty, and countless other publications. But what matters most? The couples, families, and brands who trust us to tell their stories.
