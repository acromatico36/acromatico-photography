Sizes

Our album sizes range from the majestic 16×20" (40×50cm) to the classic 12×16" (30×40cm) and the compact 9.5×13" (25×35cm) and 8×12" (20×30cm). Landscape and square versions are also available, all are flush mount panoramic.

Smaller Copies

You can clone your main album into smaller copies, flush mount as well, ideal for parents and friends: 4×5" (10×13cm), 6×8" (15×20cm), 8×12" (20×30cm) vertical, square and landscape. Or even smaller copies stitch bound: 2×2.5" (4×6cm), 2.5×4" (6×10cm), 4×5" (10×13cm) vertical, square and landscape.

The Covers: Where the Magic Begins

Creating the cover is one of the most exciting moments in the album design process. We have always offered an immense richness of materials and customizations. In order to guarantee uniqueness and exclusivity, our covers are all handmade in single copy, and you can easily define every detail with our design team. Over 180 cover materials to choose from.
